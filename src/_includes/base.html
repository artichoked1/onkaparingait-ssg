Capabilities

Onkaparinga IT offers a wide range of IT help and repair services. We can help with Windows, MacOS, and Linux systems. We specialise in the following:

General IT Help

  • System-clean-up and upgrades
  • Software installation and troubleshooting
  • Email setup and help
  • Set up printers and other peripherals
  • Home visits for home users and small businesses
  • Wi-Fi setup and troubleshooting

PC and laptop Repair

  • General hardware diagnostics

Backup and Recovery

Advanced Setups

  • NAS setups
